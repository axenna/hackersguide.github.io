About Me

Hi I'm Zenna! Welcome to my blog: A Hackers Guide to the Galaxy. A little about me: I am a freshman computer science major attending The University of Texas at Dallas and I am a hobbyist software engineer and open source enthusiast. I have a range of interests from functional programming to Extended Berkley Packet Filters and cyber security. This blog is where I write about those interests sometimes... Today I do a lot of programming in Rust and Haskell. I think expressive type systems are beautiful and love languages with them. If you would like to reach out to me, please do at: zennaallwein@gmail.com And if you would like to help support my content, I do enjoy coffee.
